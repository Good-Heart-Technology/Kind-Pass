About Kind Pass

Kind Pass generates easy-to-type passphrases that are both secure and convenient. Our passphrases are 16 characters long and use a combination of words and numbers that are easy to remember and type while maintaining security.

Why Passphrases Matter

Passphrases are more secure than traditional passwords because they're longer and easier to remember. They're also more resistant to brute-force attacks and dictionary-based cracking attempts.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is important to us. All passphrases are generated directly in your browser using JavaScript - no data is ever sent to or stored on our servers. We recommend using a password manager to securely store your passphrases.

Additional Security Tips

While strong passphrases are important, they're just one part of good security practices. We strongly recommend using Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) whenever possible. Learn more about MFA from CISA.
